Welcome!

This is my Creative Project for ENGL 1601!

I've chosen two of my favorite pieces of media and will be discussing them. included in each discussion will be:

  • A brief summary
  • What I believe the purpose of the story is
  • Likely influences/connections
  • Cultural and societal relevance
  • Narrative strategies
  • Why I picked the story and how it relates to me
  • A quote I like

Feel free to look around! If you have any questions, they might be answered in About Me.
